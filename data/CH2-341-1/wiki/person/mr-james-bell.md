A minister whose exact charge is one of this volume's flagged open questions: the
transcription notes explicitly confirm **Bell and Mr James Blair are distinct men** (a
distinction that took several openings to pin down, since both are active in the same
period and their names are easy to conflate at a glance) [img_99–100 region].

## What the record shows

Bell preaches at the 1646 Kirkcolm visitation under the heading "Master James Bell,"
alongside the ongoing Bell-vs-Blair/Kirkcolm-vs-Portmontgomery puzzle the transcriber flags
as unresolved — it is not yet certain whether "Portmontgomery" in some visitation headings
is a genuine separate charge or an alias/misreading for Kirkcolm [img_100, img_0055 region].
