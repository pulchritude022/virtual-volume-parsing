A parish whose vacancy after the death of **Mr John Porterfield** (by November 1642) runs in
parallel with the Power case, and which is finally filled by **Mr James Blair**, admitted 3
January 1644 [img_53].

## An unresolved place-name puzzle

The transcription notes flag a genuine, still-open question here: a visitation heading reads
"kirk of Portmontgomery," yet the minister examined at it is Mr James Blair, who was
admitted to Kirkcolm — suggesting "Portmontgomery" may be an alias or misreading for
Kirkcolm in at least some folios, rather than a wholly separate parish. This has not been
resolved against an independent gazetteer and is flagged for review rather than asserted.
