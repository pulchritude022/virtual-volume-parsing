## Notes & context
★ **The 1650 witch-hunt reaches the Rhins pulpits.** A notable resolution: **the brethren are "to use diligence in enquiring anent those under the scandal of witchcraft or charming within their several parishes"** — the great 1649–50 Scottish witch-panic prompting the presbytery to open a fresh parish-by-parish enquiry (echoing the 1644 witch processes of imgs 64–65 and the Aug-1649 commission of img 175). To watch as the corpus continues.

★ **The Engagement penitents keep coming.** **William Baillie, a Lieutenant of horse**, supplicates to profess repentance for the Engagement; **Patrick McKie** (the 1648 Engager, imgs 152, 177) supplicates to be admitted to the Covenant — the reconciliation track absorbing ever more of the local Engagement soldiery.

★ **A knotty paternity/uncleanness case:** **Marion**, accused with one **Sebastian**, fugitive under scandal of uncleanness, gives a shifting, "very legal" deposition (as if coached), out-fathering her child; **Andrew Kennedy of Arisemman [Airieman]** is also suspected — Scott to deal with them. Plus the post-Synod housekeeping of the 22 May sitting (exercise, elder admissions, the Sheriff-younger as Stoneykirk's ruling elder).
