Parish of **Mr Alexander Turnbull**, one of the presbytery's most active clerks and
moderators. Kirkmaiden's own visitations follow the standard presbytery form — minister and
elders examined separately on doctrine and discipline, the session book checked — and one
records the heritors promising to refund Turnbull's own glebe outlay after his death, a small
sign of a minister well regarded by his own parish [img_98].
